three ways to get one

  • Buy itOwn it now. Pay upfront, or pay monthly through a financer at checkout (real rates shown there). Keep it at home, or have us host it.
  • Own it over timeNothing upfront. We host and run it while you pay it off — mining to your wallet the whole time. Paid off, it's yours: keep it hosted, or we ship it.
  • Just rent oneDon't want to own hardware? Rent a dedicated rig month-to-month — we house, power, and run it; it mines 100% to your wallet; cancel anytime.

Exact monthly numbers post with the store. All three mine to your own wallet; none of them is an investment product — you're paying for a machine and, if you want it, a place to run it.
