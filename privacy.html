Privacy.

We ask for as little as we can, and we use it for one thing.

what we collect

When you reserve, we collect your email address and the product, membership, and accessory choices you pick. Nothing else. No tracking, no analytics, no cookies, no accounts, no passwords.

why

Only to contact you about your reservation when the store opens. That's the whole reason it exists.

how it's handled

The reservation form sends it through Web3Forms, a form relay, to our own inbox on Proton. We don't sell it, we don't share it, and we don't use it for anything else.

your control

Reserving is non-binding and free.

Ask us to delete you anytime at daarxmoney@proton.me, and you're removed.

The fully private, self-hosted store comes at mainnet. Until then, this relay is how reservations reach us.
