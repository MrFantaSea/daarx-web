L10N

The one designed to spend. Private-cash and offline-transfer goals, not a live network or available payment product.

How it works ↓ System status →

why a second coin

A store-of-value design and a spending design want different economics. L10N is designed to move. Its shared-chain, provisional emission/genesis, and merged-mining acceptance paths are implemented and locally tested, while exact timing and monetary parameters remain provisional.

the cash part

The target is private phone cash with an optional offline layer. Offline value is difficult: devices, replay protection, fraud limits, recovery, settlement, and merchant risk all matter. The current app work is not a production wallet and offline transfer is not approved for public use.

The proposed name for L10N's smallest unit is a tooth. This page covers the L10N protocol only; L10N$HARE and Rally are separate products and will have a separate public property.

No signal?research and test target

An offline layer could be released only after it passes protocol, device, fraud, recovery, and real-world tests. Today it has not earned that claim.

what's not settled

The exact block cadence and emission numbers must be measured, not guessed. A future public test network is intended to produce evidence before parameters are frozen; no such public network exists today.

Status — July 15, 2026: L10N's core integration is active. Early offline CTS-B consensus primitives exist, but the complete offline protocol, wallet/device layer, public testnet, payment product, and final whitepaper are not released. No release date is promised.
Private cash designed to be mined.

The intended launch policy starts at zero with no premine, presale, or coin sale. Merged mining and the release wallet must be verified before anyone can actually earn or spend L10N.
