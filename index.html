by design

The private ones don't know your name. The public one can't hide a thing.

DAARX and L10N have no company behind them — nothing to buy, no one to pressure. OPEN has books — and they're public. Keeping those two things separate isn't an accident. It's the whole point.

One of them works when nothing else does.

No power, no signal, off the grid — cards stop, banks stop, Bitcoin stops. This keeps going.
