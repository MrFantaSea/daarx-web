01 / THE TRUST BOUNDARY

Privacy should be infrastructure.

A transparent base ledger can leave addresses, histories, and payment relationships open to analysis. DAARX and L10N target a different default: private value for people. OPEN explores public accounting for machines.

RELATIONSHIPS REMAIN LEGIBLE

RELATIONSHIPS ARE THE PRIVACY TARGET

MODEL / TRANSPARENT BASE LEDGER

The ledger remembers.

Public transaction histories can make relationships visible long after a payment. Pseudonyms alone are not the same thing as privacy.

MODEL / PRIVACY DESIGN TARGET

The relationship is the secret.

DAARX and L10N target hidden amounts and full-chain membership proofs. This is an anonymity-set design, not a guarantee of end-to-end anonymity.

The glow is visual. The limits are real.
