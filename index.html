Privacy for people. Accountability for machines.

Two planned coins and one future accountability protocol. DAARX and L10N target private payments; OPEN is designed to make machine accounts auditable. These are design goals, not live-network claims.

by design

Private for people. Auditable for machines.

DAARX and L10N are designed with no central issuer. Desiderata Labs LLC separately operates this site and may operate future hardware or support services; no hardware offer exists today, and any future hardware would be separate from coins and protocol control. OPEN is a future public-accounting design, not a live ledger.

off the grid

One of them aims to work when nothing else does.

Offline L10N is a research and test goal, not an available feature. It must pass protocol, fraud, replay, device, recovery, and real-world tests before it can be described as usable without a network.
