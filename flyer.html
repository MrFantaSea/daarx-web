Savings coin
DAARX

Targets hidden amounts and an exact integer emission schedule that eventually ends. Unlaunched; under development and internal review.

Spending coin
L10N

Targets private phone cash and a possible offline layer. No public wallet, payment network, or offline release exists.

Research for people who believe financial privacy matters. Privacy can fail through software, metadata, endpoints, or new cryptanalysis. These unfinished protocols are not usable payment alternatives today and may never launch.
