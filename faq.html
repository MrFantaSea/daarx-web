the questions

Is this an investment? Will I make money?

No — and it isn't a security either. There's no sale, no issuer, and no promised return: you mine coins that start at zero, to take part and hold a stake early. Early on, your power can cost more than you mine, and there's no guaranteed income. You do it to own a stake early and help secure the network. Nothing here is financial advice.

Are the coins for sale?

Never. No premine, no presale, no token sale. Mining is the only way in, for everyone. The rigs are separate hardware; buying one gets you a computer, not coins.

Do I need to be technical?

No. The plug-and-go image does the setup: flash it (or buy it pre-flashed), plug in, it mines. Or buy a built rig and skip all of it.

Is the software really free?

Yes. The node and miner are free and open source, always. You only pay for hardware and convenience if you want them.

When does it ship? When can I mine?

The network isn't live yet. Testnet late 2026, mainnet target March 16, 2027, only if it's ready. Reservations are open now; the real store and shipping come with the network, and nothing is charged until then.

What's the catch?

There isn't a hidden one. The coins are worth nothing at launch, the software is free, and we say so plainly. If you're here for guaranteed profit, this isn't it. If you're here to get in early and help build something honest, it is.

Why buy a rig instead of building my own?

You don't have to. The image and the parts list are free. Buy a rig only if you'd rather we handle the building, testing, and support.

Is there a node-only option?

Yes. The Node Box runs a full node without mining, around $299. It earns nothing; it helps secure and decentralize the network.

What happens to my info when I reserve?

We collect your email and what you're interested in, only to reach you when the store opens. We don't sell or share it, and you can drop off anytime. See the privacy page.
